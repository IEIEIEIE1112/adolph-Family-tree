Adolph Family Legacy

Joseph Patrick Adolph & Agnes Gunn

Joseph Patrick Adolph (1882–1958) and Agnes Gunn (1893–1971) married and lived near Nespelem, Washington. They were Colville Confederated Tribes members (Agnes of the Lakes band) and raised their family on the reservation. Joseph served on the tribal business council. Tribal records (Naomi Price obituary) confirm that Joseph and Agnes are the great-grandparents of Naomi Price:contentReference[oaicite:27]{index=27}, linking this family tree.

Children of Joseph & Agnes

  • Eva Adolph (1911–2006): Married Lionel Orr. A respected Sinixt elder, Eva spearheaded repatriation of ancestral remains across the BC border:contentReference[oaicite:28]{index=28}. Her children included Vance “Bob” Campbell Sr. (Sinixt headman):contentReference[oaicite:29]{index=29}:contentReference[oaicite:30]{index=30} and Dolores “Lola” Campbell (who in 2005 gave birth to Agnice Sophia Campbell, noted as the first Sinixt birth in their territory in decades:contentReference[oaicite:31]{index=31}).
  • William “Shortie” Adolph (1914–1963): Born June 22, 1914 in Stevens County, WA, William served as a U.S. Army PFC in WWII. He died July 1, 1963 and is buried in Wenatchee, WA:contentReference[oaicite:32]{index=32}.
  • Anna “Annie” Adolph (1916–1916): Died in infancy on Sept. 2, 1916 (Danville, WA).
  • Marie Mary Adolph (1918–1978): Married George Goujon (Nespelem). In records she appears as “Marie Adolph Goujon”:contentReference[oaicite:33]{index=33}. Their son Kenneth married Matilda Timentwa; their daughter Naomi (Price) later confirmed Joseph and Agnes as Naomi’s great-grandparents:contentReference[oaicite:34]{index=34}.
  • Ambrose Adolph (1921–2012): Born Nov. 21, 1921 in Boyds, WA; died Mar. 2, 2012 in Disautel, WA:contentReference[oaicite:35]{index=35}. A WWII U.S. Army veteran (landed on D-Day), Ambrose was a Colville tribal member (Arrow Lakes band). He married Julia Lucille Weed (1927–1997).
  • Vincent Randolph “Beanie” Adolph (1923–1964): Born 1923; died Oct. 25, 1964 (Idaho). Vincent served in the military and married Iris Ione Penley. Their daughter was Arlene Bertilla Adolph (1953–1997).

Grandchildren

  • Vance Robert “Bob” Campbell Sr.: Son of Eva. Became Headman of the Sinixt (Arrow Lakes) people:contentReference[oaicite:36]{index=36}:contentReference[oaicite:37]{index=37}.
  • Dolores “Lola” Campbell: Daughter of Eva; married Delmar Lezime. Mother of Agnice Sophia Campbell.

  • Naomi Anita Price (1964–2005): Daughter of Kenneth; member of Colville Tribes. Her obituary lists Joseph & Agnes as her great-grandparents:contentReference[oaicite:38]{index=38}. She married Michael Price and had three children.
  • Arlene (Adolph) Zacherle (1953–1997): Daughter of Vincent; married Carl Zacherle.
