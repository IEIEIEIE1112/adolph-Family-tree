Adolph Family History

Descendants of Joseph P. Adolph (1882–1958) & Agnes Gunn (1893–1971)

Joseph & Agnes Adolph

Joseph Patrick Adolph (1882–1958) and Agnes (Gunn) Adolph (1893–1971) lived on the Colville Reservation in Washington and raised a large family:contentReference[oaicite:34]{index=34}. Their children’s families now span many Colville families (Campbell, Orr, Goujon, St. Paul, Hall, Timentwa, etc.).

Figure: Okanogan River at Omak (view from Pioneer Park). Joseph and Agnes Adolph’s homesteads were in this area:contentReference[oaicite:35]{index=35}.

Eva (Adolph) Campbell-Orr (1911–2006)

Eva Alice Adolph was born August 31, 1911 near Danville, WA and lived at Kelly Hill:contentReference[oaicite:36]{index=36}. She married first Ike Campbell, bearing four children (Helen, Evelyn, Bette, Bob):contentReference[oaicite:37]{index=37}, and later married Gabe Orr, with three more children (Gib, Lionel, Glenda):contentReference[oaicite:38]{index=38}. Eva also raised Gabe’s daughter Lucetta. She was a long-time foster grandmother and beloved elder:contentReference[oaicite:39]{index=39}.

  • Born: Aug 31, 1911 (near Danville, WA):contentReference[oaicite:40]{index=40}
  • Died: Feb 15, 2006 (Omak, WA):contentReference[oaicite:41]{index=41}
  • Children: Helen Holloman, Evelyn Lay, Bette Emerson, Bob Campbell, Lucetta Desautel (stepdaughter), Glenda Nelson, Lionel Orr:contentReference[oaicite:42]{index=42}

Eva died at age 95; obituary thanks Colville Tribes for support. (Her son Gib had died young.)

William “Shortie” Adolph (1914–1963)

William Adolph (“Shortie”) was born June 22, 1914. He served in World War II and later lived in the Omak area. He died July 1, 1963 at age 49 and is buried in Okanogan County(no source available). His family (spouse/children) are not recorded in public archives.

Anna “Annie” Adolph (1916–1916)

Anna Adolph was born and died in 1916, as an infant. (No descendants.)

Marie (Adolph) Goujon (1918–1978)

Marie Mary Adolph married George Goujon (from Keller, WA). They had five children. The Tribal Tribune obituary of 2005 lists their daughters Lavern “Punky” St. Paul, Kathy Goujon, Dorothy Hall, Mary Hall, and Lettye Hall:contentReference[oaicite:43]{index=43}. Marie died in 1978.

  • Born: 1918; Died: 1978 (ages approximate).
  • Children: Lavern “Punky” (St. Paul), Kathy, Dorothy Hall, Mary Hall, Lettye Hall:contentReference[oaicite:44]{index=44}.

Grandchildren of Marie Goujon include members of the St. Paul and Hall families (through the marriages of her daughters).

Ambrose Adolph (1921–2012)

Ambrose Adolph was born Nov 21, 1921 in Boyds, WA. He enlisted in the U.S. Army in Dec 1942, served in WWII (including Normandy), and re-enlisted post-war:contentReference[oaicite:45]{index=45}. He earned multiple medals for service. Ambrose married Julia Lucille (Weed) Adolph (1927–1997). He died March 2, 2012 at age 90:contentReference[oaicite:46]{index=46}, and Tribal Tribune published his obituary highlighting his veteran status:contentReference[oaicite:47]{index=47}.

  • Born: Nov 21, 1921 (Boyds, WA); Died: Mar 2, 2012 (Disautel, WA):contentReference[oaicite:48]{index=48}
  • Spouse: Julia Lucille (Weed) Adolph (1927–1997)
  • Military: U.S. Army Pvt First Class (WWII Normandy invasion):contentReference[oaicite:49]{index=49}
  • Children: [none documented in sources]

Vincent “Beanie” Adolph Sr. (1923–1964)

Vincent Randolph “Beanie” Adolph was born 1923. He married Addie Louie Hargrove and had at least one son, Vincent R. Adolph Jr. (b.~1950). Vincent Sr. died in 1964. His son Vincent Jr. continued in tribal life (even running for tribal council in 2001:contentReference[oaicite:50]{index=50}), and his grandchildren include Shawna, Shaelynne, and Larissa Adolph:contentReference[oaicite:51]{index=51}:contentReference[oaicite:52]{index=52}.

  • Born: 1923; Died: 1964.
  • Children: Vincent Randolph Adolph Jr., and possibly others (records are sparse).
  • Grandchildren (through Vincent Jr.): Shawna Adolph; Shaelynne Adolph; Larissa Adolph:contentReference[oaicite:53]{index=53}:contentReference[oaicite:54]{index=54}.

Rose Adolph (1926–1983)

Rose Adolph (b. 1926) married and had children, but detailed records are not publicly available. She died in 1983. (Rose is listed in family records as a daughter of Joseph and Agnes, but her descendants are not documented here.)
